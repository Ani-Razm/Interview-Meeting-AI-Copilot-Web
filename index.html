Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Rakaia™ and how it can benefit your workflow.

What is Rakaia™?

Rakaia™ is an advanced AI assistant designed to optimize how you work. It enhances your productivity by providing real-time insights, automating repetitive tasks, and offering tailored feedback to improve your remote meetings and interviews.

How does Rakaia™ work?

Rakaia™ integrates seamlessly with your communication tools, such as Zoom and Slack, to assist during meetings. It transcribes conversations, highlights key points, and suggests actionable next steps, all in real-time. Think of it as your personal assistant that never misses a detail.

Is my data secure with Rakaia™?

Yes! Rakaia™ follows industry-leading privacy practices. Your data is processed locally on your device through edge computing, ensuring that sensitive information never leaves your network. We prioritize your privacy and guarantee your data remains secure at all times.

Can I customize my answers with Rakaia™?

Absolutely! Rakaia™ allows you to personalize your responses to match your style, tone, and experience. Whether you're preparing for an interview or a meeting, you can adjust the AI's suggestions to sound more like you, ensuring the answers are both natural and impactful.
